Company
Overview

CIS Autobody & spraypainters is a 100% black owned private limited company. It was instituted on the base of providing better and quality services to the community. With road accidents being on the upsurge, CIS believes that one of the roots of the aforementioned is poor vehicle conditions. Therefore, with our expertise we ensure our customers, peace of mind and comfortable drive after leaving our premises. We provide you with quality services at an affordable price.

Company
Description

CIS is a professional Panel Beating company that was established in 2022 by professionally trained, experienced and qualified directors: Mr. Ian Letlalo, Mr. Shaun Dube, Mr. Clive Maphosa. Panel Beating is a field which requires a good deal of knowledge and skill. Thus, we are comprised of skilled and experienced workmanship. Before we commence, we thoroughly assess the extent of the damage to a vehicle, and whether it is cost effective and safe to attempt to repair it. We treat all our customers with respect and professional approach to ensure all repairs revamp the vehicle to its former glory. We are strongly against any form of corruption and we respect local customs. The company is led by experienced director who pursue job creation and empowerment by encouraging equal opportunities regardless of race, religion nor gender. At CIS we go an extra mile to live up to our core values: professionalism, reliability and integrity.

Our Services

Panel Beating

We are a trusted repairer in Edenvale and are based on mutually beneficial relationships. We have high standards of workmanship & customer service.

Expert Chassis Straightening

We have dedicated staff and workshop space to straighten chassis of any vehicle brand or type. We take pride in bringing your car back to the state it was before an accident.

Insurance Claim Assistance

We assist you in the process and will follow up your claim with your insurance company including managing the assessment process, liaising with the insurance assessor and others until your claim approval is received.

Professional Spray Painting

We have all the equipment required such as Spray Booth and experienced personnel for all types of vehicles and do a complete recoat on any brand or touch-ups for dent or panel damage.

Full Auto Body Polishing

Using our experienced staff and fully fitted facilities we perform full auto body polishing to excellent standards.

Damage Repair

We repair non-structural damage as quickly and efficient as possible.
